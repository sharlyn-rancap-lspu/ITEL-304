Korean Cuisine
Background:

"Experience that Korean zing!" Known for having one of the most healthiest dishes on Earth, Koreans tend to have their meals low in fat, and high in nutrition. Their food is said to have a careful consideration of balance in temperature, spiciness, colour and texture, along with considered presentation. And also known for Koreans enthusiastically love eating spicy meals, they just don't eat tangy foods on a daily basis.
